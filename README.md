# OpenCommunity.Directory

**Discover and list online communities.** → **[opencommunity.directory](https://opencommunity.directory)**

A modern, SEO-optimized directory for finding and submitting online communities across Discord, Slack, Telegram, WhatsApp, Reddit and more — one searchable home for the communities worth joining.

## What it does

- 🔎 **Browse & search** communities by platform, topic, and size.
- ➕ **Submit a community** — owners list their Discord/Slack/Telegram/etc. to reach new members.
- 🖼️ **Rich listings** with logos, descriptions, and auto-generated social preview images.
- 🔐 **Sign in with Google or GitHub** to manage listings.

## Who it's for

People looking for active online communities to join, and community owners who want to grow their membership.

## Tech stack

Next.js (App Router, TypeScript) · Tailwind CSS + shadcn/ui · PostgreSQL + Prisma · Auth.js · Cloudinary · Vercel + Neon
